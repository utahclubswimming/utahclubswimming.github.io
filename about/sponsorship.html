Sponsorship

How you can help fund our team

Each year, our team accepts sponsorships from companies, families, and individuals to help fund the ongoing costs of running the team. Money raised from sponsorship is used for paying for pool facilities for the season, operational expenses, basic team apparel, league membership dues, and various other expenses for the team.
