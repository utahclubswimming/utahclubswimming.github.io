Practice Schedule

When to come swim with us

Our practices are at the George S. Eccles Student Life Center and the HPER Natatorium (which is also home to the school's NCAA team). Unless otherwise noted, practices take place in the pool listed below. You can generally find us on the pool deck about ten minutes in advance of the start of practice. Dryland practices usually meet at the track in the Student Life Center, but the final location may change depending on the workout as determined by the day's workout leader (pay attention to our official communications channels for more info on these).

The Student Life Center is located just below Legacy Bridge and north of the Marriott Honors Community.

The HPER Natatorium is to the south and west of the Student Life Center at the south end of the HPER complex.
